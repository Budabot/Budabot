To show all players on the friendlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>friendlist<end>

To search the friendlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>friendlist <i>search</i><end>

To remove unneeded players on the friendlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>friendlist clean<end>

To add a player to the friendlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>addbuddy <i>name</i> <i>type</i><end>

To remove a player from the friendlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>rembuddy <i>name</i> <i>type</i><end>

To remove all characters from the friendlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>rembuddyall<end>
