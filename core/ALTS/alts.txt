<highlight>To show your own alts:<end>
<tab><symbol>alts

<highlight>To show alts from other users:<end>
<tab><symbol>alts 'name'

<highlight>To add characters as your alts:<end>
<tab><symbol>alts add 'alt1' 'alt2' 'alt3'

<highlight>To add yourself as an alt of a character:<end>
<tab><symbol>alts main 'name'

<highlight>To choose one of your alts to be your new main:<end>
<tab><symbol>alts setmain 'name'

<highlight>To remove alts from your list:<end>
<tab><symbol>alts rem 'alt'
