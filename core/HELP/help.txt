To see a list of all help topics:
<highlight><tab><symbol>help<end>

To see a list of all help topics for a module:
<highlight><tab><symbol>help 'module name'<end>

To see a help topic:
<highlight><tab><symbol>help 'topic'<end>

To see the help topic for a command:
<highlight><tab><symbol>help 'command'<end>
