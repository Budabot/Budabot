<highlight>To show a list of the currently banned characters:<end>
<tab><symbol>banlist

<highlight>To permanent ban a character from the bot (without a reason specified):<end>
<tab><symbol>ban 'playername'

<highlight>To remove a character from the banlist:<end>
<tab><symbol>unban 'playername'

<highlight>To temporarily ban a character from the bot (without a reason specified):<end>
<tab><symbol>ban 'time' 'playername'

<tab><highlight>For example:<end>
<tab><symbol>ban 2weeks badplayer
<tab>(bans badplayer for 2weeks from the bot)

<highlight>To temporarily ban a character from the bot (with a reason specified):<end>
<tab><symbol>ban 'time' 'playername' reason 'reason for the ban'

<tab><highlight>For example:<end>
<tab><symbol>ban 2weeks badplayer reason Ninjalooter
<tab>(bans badplayer for 2 weeks from the bot with the reason 'Ninjalooter')

<highlight>To permanently ban a character from the bot (with a reason specified):<end>
<tab><symbol>ban 'playername' reason 'reason for the ban'

<tab><highlight>For example:<end>
<tab><symbol>ban badplayer reason Ninjalooter
<tab>(permanently bans badplayer from the bot with the reason 'Ninjalooter')

See <a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> help budatime'><symbol>help budatime</a> for info on the format of the 'time' parameter.
