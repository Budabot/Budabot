To show the list of all events and their statuses:
<highlight><tab><symbol>eventlist<end>

To show the list of all events of a certain type and their statuses:
<highlight><tab><symbol>eventlist <i>event_type</i><end>

Valid event types are: msg, priv, extpriv, guild, joinpriv, leavepriv, orgmsg, extjoinprivrequest, logon, logoff, towers, connect, sendguild, sendpriv, setup, and packet(id).
