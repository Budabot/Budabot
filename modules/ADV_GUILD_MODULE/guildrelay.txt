<header>::::: Help on Guildrelay Commands. :::::<end>

To be able to relay the chats between two guilds both bots must be a Budabot and also the bots needs to share the database.
Read the manual on how to share the database for 2 bots.

<highlight>To turn on the guildrelay<end>
<tab><symbol>guildrelay 'Insert other botname'

Do this command on both bots or it will not work.
For example relaying guildchat between bot1 and bot2 do

On bot1
<tab><symbol>guildrelay bot2
and on bot2
<tab><symbol>guildrelay bot1

<highlight>To disable the guildrelay<end>
<tab><symbol>guildrelay off
