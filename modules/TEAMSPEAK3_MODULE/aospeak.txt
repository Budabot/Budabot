To show all players AOSpeak server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>aospeak<end>

To show org members currently connected to the AOSpeak server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>aospeak org<end>
Note: Before you can use this command your org president must set up an org channel on the AOSpeak server.

To show all players from RK1 on AOSpeak server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>aospeak 1<end>

To show all players from RK2 on AOSpeak server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>aospeak 2<end>
