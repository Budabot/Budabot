To show which and how much your skills will increase by increasing an ability:
<highlight><tab><symbol>trickle 'ability' 'amount'<end>

<highlight>Example:<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>trickle agi 20<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>trickle agi 20 intel 40 psychic 60<end>

Valid abilities are: agi, int, psy, sta, str, sen
