<header> :::::: Trickle Command :::::: <end>

<highlight>To show which and how much your skills will increase by increasing an ability:<end>
<tab><symbol>trickle 'ability' 'amount'

<highlight>Example:<end>
<tab><symbol>trickle agi 20
<tab><symbol>trickle agi 20 intel 40 psychic 60

Valid abilities are: agi, int, psy, sta, str, sen
