To see the last 20 attack messages:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks<end>

To see page 2 of the last attack messages:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks 'page number'<end>

To see the last 20 attack messages for a certain site:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks 'playfield' 'site number'<end>

To see page 2 of the last attack messages for a certain site:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks 'playfield' 'site number' 'page number'<end>

To see the last 20 attack messages for specific person:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks player 'character name'<end>

To see page 2 of the last attack messages for specific person:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks player 'character name' 'page number'<end>

To see the last 20 attack messages for specific guild:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks org 'exact guild name'<end>

To see page 2 of the last attack messages for specific guild:
<highlight><tab><symbol>attacks org 'exact guild name' 'page number'<end>

Note: you can use '%' as a wildcard in guild and player names
