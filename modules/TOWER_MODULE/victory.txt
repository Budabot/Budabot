To see the last 20 victory messages:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory<end>

To see page 2 of the last victory messages:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory 'page number'<end>

To see the last 20 victory messages for a certain site:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory 'playfield' 'site number'<end>

To see page 2 of the last victory messages for a certain site:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory 'playfield' 'site number' 'page number'<end>

To see the last 20 victory messages for specific person:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory player 'player name'<end>

To see page 2 of the last victory messages for specific person:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory player 'player name' 'page number'<end>

To see the last 20 victory messages for specific guild:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory org 'exact guild name'<end>

To see page 2 of the last victory messages for specific guild:
<highlight><tab><symbol>victory org 'exact guild name' 'page number'<end>

Note: you can use '%' as a wildcard in guild and player names
