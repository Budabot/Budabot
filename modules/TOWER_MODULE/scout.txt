<header> :::::: Scout Commands :::::: <end>

<highlight>To scout a tower site :<end>
<tab><symbol>scout 'playfield' 'site number' 'closing time' 'ct ql' 'faction' 'guild name'

For instance:
<tab><symbol>scout PW 4 12:34:56 190 clan Dark Ninjas

<highlight>To remove a scouted tower site:<end>
<tab><symbol>remscout 'playfield' 'site number'

<highlight>To show info on the list of scouted bases:<end>
<tab><symbol>opentimes
