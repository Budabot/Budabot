To shhow info about an org on the current server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>whoisorg 'org id'<end>

To show info about a player's org on the current server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>whoisorg 'player'<end>

To show info about an org on a specified server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>whoisorg 'org id' 'server number'<end>

To show info about a player's org on a specified server:
<highlight><tab><symbol>whoisorg 'player' 'server number'<end>
