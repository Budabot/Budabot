To invite a player to the private channel:
<highlight><tab><symbol>invite 'player'<end>

To kick a player from the private channel
<highlight><tab><symbol>kick 'player'<end>

To add a player to the guestlist(he will get auto-invited after he logs on):
<highlight><tab><symbol>adduser 'player'<end>

To remove a player from the guestlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>remuser 'player'<end>

To show who is on the guestlist:
<highlight><tab><symbol>members<end>

To join the private channel:
<highlight><tab><symbol>join<end>

To leave the private channel:
<highlight><tab><symbol>leave<end>
