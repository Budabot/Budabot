<header>::::: Land Control Commands :::::<end>

<highlight>To see info about all sites in a playfield<end>
<tab><symbol>lc 'playfield'
<green><tab><symbol>lc GTC<end>

<highlight>To see a info about a specific site<end>
<tab><symbol>lc 'playfield' 'site_number'
<green><tab><symbol>lc GTC 3<end>

<highlight>To see a info about sites belonging to a specific org<end>
<tab><symbol>lc org 'org_name'
<green><tab><symbol>lc org Devil Inside<end>
