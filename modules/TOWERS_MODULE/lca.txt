<header>::::: Help on Land Control Areas :::::<end>

<highlight>To search after LCA´s with a specific QL:<end>
<tab><symbol>lca 'ql'

For example:
<tab><symbol>lca 76


<highlight>To search after LCA´s with in a specific zone:<end>
<tab><symbol>lca 'zone'

For example:
<tab><symbol>lca newland


<highlight>To search after LCA´s with in a specific zone and ql:<end>
<tab><symbol>lca 'ql' 'zone'

For example:
<tab><symbol>lca 20 newland
