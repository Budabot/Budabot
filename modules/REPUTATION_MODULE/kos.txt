To see a list of characters on the kill-on-sight list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>kos<end>

To add a character to the kill-on-sight list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>kos add 'character'<end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>kos add 'character' 'comment'<end>

To remove a character from the kill-on-sight list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>kos rem 'character'<end>
