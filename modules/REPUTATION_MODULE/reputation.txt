To see reputation for a player:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'player'<end>

To see all reputation for a player:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'player' all<end>

To add positive reputation for a player:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'player' +1 'reason'<end>

To add negative reputation for a player:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'player' -1 'reason'<end>
