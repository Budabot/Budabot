To see a list of characters that have reputation:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation<end>

To see reputation for a character:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'character'<end>

To see all reputation for a character:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'character' all<end>

To add positive reputation for a character:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'character' +1 'reason'<end>

To add negative reputation for a character:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reputation 'character' -1 'reason'<end>
