To see the current rally:
<highlight><tab><symbol>rally<end>

To set the rally:
<highlight><tab><symbol>rally <i>x</i> <i>y</i> <i>playfield</i><end>
<highlight><tab><symbol>rally <i>paste_coords</i><end>

To clear the rally:
<highlight><tab><symbol>rally clear<end>

Note: <i>playfield</i> can be the playfield id, or any of the short or long names defined in <symbol>playfields.
