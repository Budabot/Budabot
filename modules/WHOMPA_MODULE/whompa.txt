<header> ::::: Whompa Commands ::::: <end>

<highlight>To see the list whompa cities:<end>
<tab><symbol>whompa

<highlight>To see the list of destinations for a whompa city:<end>
<tab><symbol>whompa 'city'

<highlight>To see a whompa path from one city to another:<end>
<tab><symbol>whompa 'city1' 'city2'
