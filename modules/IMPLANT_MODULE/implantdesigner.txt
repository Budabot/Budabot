To see your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner<end>

To see a specified slot in your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner <i>slot</i><end>

To add a cluster in a specified slot for your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner <i>slot</i> <i>grade</i> <i>cluster</i><end>

To set the QL for a specified slot in your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner <i>slot</i> <i>QL</i><end>

To see the result of your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner <i>results</i><end>

To remove all clusters from your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner clear<end>

To clear all clusters from a specified slot in your current implant design:
<highlight><tab><symbol>implantdesigner <i>slot</i> clear<end>

*<i>slot</i> can be one of: head, eye, ear, rarm, chest, larm, rwrist, waist, lwrist, rhand, legs, lhand, feet.
**<i>grade</i> can be one of: shiny, bright faded.
***<i>cluster</i> can be any skill name (exactly only).

By default, <highlight><symbol>impdesign<end> is provided as an alias for <highlight><symbol>implantdesigner<end>.
