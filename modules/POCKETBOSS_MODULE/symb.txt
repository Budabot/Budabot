To show a list of symbiants and which pocketboss drops it:
<highlight><tab><symbol>symb 'slotname' 'type'<end>

To show all symbiants of a certain type:
<highlight><tab><symbol>symb 'type'<end>

To show all symbiants which go in a certain slot:
<highlight><tab><symbol>symb 'slot'<end>
