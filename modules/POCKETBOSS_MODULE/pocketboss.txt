<header>::::: Pocketboss Commands :::::<end>

<highlight>To show a list of Symbiants that a Pocketboss do<end>
<tab><symbol>pb 'Insert Pocketbossname'

<highlight>To show a list of Symbiants and which PB it drops do<end>
<tab><symbol>symb 'Insert Slotname' 'Insert Type'

Allowed Slotnames
<tab>- eye
<tab>- head
<tab>- ear
<tab>- rarm
<tab>- chest
<tab>- larm
<tab>- rwrist
<tab>- waist
<tab>- lwrist
<tab>- rhand
<tab>- legs
<tab>- lhand
<tab>- feet

Allowed Types
<tab>- support
<tab>- control
<tab>- infantry
<tab>- artillery
<tab>- extermination
