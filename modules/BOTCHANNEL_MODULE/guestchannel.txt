<header>::::: Help on guestchannel :::::<end>

<highlight>To show who is in the guest channel do<end>
<tab><symbol>onlineguests

<highlight>To invite someone to guestchannel<end>
<tab><symbol>inviteuser 'insert name here'

<highlight>To kick someone from guestchannel<end>
<tab><symbol>kickuser 'insert name here'

<highlight>To add someone to the guestlist(he will get auto-invited after he logs on)<end>
<tab><symbol>adduser 'insert name here'

<highlight>To remove someone from the guestlist<end>
<tab><symbol>remuser 'insert name here'

<highlight>To show the ones that are auto-invited after their logon<end>
<tab><symbol>members
