<header> ::::: Whompah Commands ::::: <end>

<highlight>To see the list whompah cities:<end>
<tab><symbol>whompah

<highlight>To see the list of destinations for a whompah city:<end>
<tab><symbol>whompah 'city'

<highlight>To see a whompah path from one city to another:<end>
<tab><symbol>whompah 'city1' 'city2'
