To search for an item:
<highlight><tab><symbol>items 'item name'<end>

To search for a specific QL of an item:
<highlight><tab><symbol>items 'QL' 'item name'<end>

To search for an item by id:
<highlight><tab><symbol>itemid 'id'<end>

Note: to not include results that match a certain search term, precede term with a minus ('-').

<header2>Examples:<end>

Shows the First tier Armor for the Nano-Technician:
<highlight><tab><symbol>items first tier nano<end>

Shows the First tier Armor for the Nano-Technician QL133
<highlight><tab><symbol>items 133 first tier nano<end>

Shows results with 'panther' but not 'ofab' in the name
<highlight><tab><symbol>items panther -ofab<end>

Shows Burden of Competence
<highlight><tab><symbol>itemid 244718<end>
