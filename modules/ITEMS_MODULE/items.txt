<highlight>To search for an item:<end>
<tab><symbol>items 'Insert itemname here'

<highlight>To search for an specific QL of an item:<end>
<tab><symbol>items 'Insert QL here' 'Insert itemname here'

<highlight>To search for an item by id:<end>
<tab><symbol>itemid 'id'

Note: To not include results that match a certain search term, precede term with a minus ('-').

<header2>Examples:<end>

<highlight>Shows the First tier Armor for the Nano-Technician<end>
<tab><symbol>items first tier nano

<highlight>Shows the First tier Armor for the Nano-Technician QL133<end>
<tab><symbol>items 133 first tier nano

<highlight>Shows results with 'panther' but not 'ofab' in the name<end>
<tab><symbol>items panther -ofab

<highlight>Shows Burden of Competence<end>
<tab><symbol>itemid 244718
