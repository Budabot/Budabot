To show a list of commands that have been silenced:
<highlight><tab><symbol>silence<end>

To silence a command:
<highlight><tab><symbol>silence 'cmd' 'channel'<end>

To unsilence a command:
<highlight><tab><symbol>unsilence 'cmd' 'channel'<end>

Note: 'channel' can be one of 'priv', 'msg', or 'guild'.
