<header>::::: Alternative Char Commands :::::<end>

<highlight>Shows your own alts<end>
<tab><symbol>alts

<highlight>Show alts from other users<end>
<tab><symbol>alts 'insert name here'

<highlight>Adding alts to your list<end>
<tab><symbol>alts add 'insert name here' 'and even another alt here'

<highlight>Setting the main of your alt (on your alt)<end>
<tab><symbol>alts main 'insert name here'

<highlight>Remove alts from your list<end>
<tab><symbol>alts rem 'insert name here'
