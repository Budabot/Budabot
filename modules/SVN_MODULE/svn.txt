To update the bot from SVN:
<highlight><tab><symbol>svn update<end>

To do a dry run update from SVN (no files actually changed):
<highlight><tab><symbol>svn dry<end>

To see information on the current SVN repository the bot was checked out from:
<highlight><tab><symbol>svn info<end>

To see SVN status on changed/added/deleted files:
<highlight><tab><symbol>svn status<end>

To see SVN status on a particular file:
<highlight><tab><symbol>svn status 'filepath'<end>
