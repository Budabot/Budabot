<highlight>To update the bot from SVN:<end>
<tab><symbol>svn update

<highlight>To do a dry run update from SVN (no files actually changed):<end>
<tab><symbol>svn dry

<highlight>To see information on the current SVN repository the bot was checked out from:<end>
<tab><symbol>svn info

<highlight>To see SVN status on changed/added/deleted files:<end>
<tab><symbol>svn status

<highlight>To see SVN status on a particular file:<end>
<tab><symbol>svn status 'filepath'
