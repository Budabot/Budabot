<header> :::::: Broadcast Module :::::: <end>

The BROADCAST_MODULE allows you to receive tells from other bots and then relay those into your guild channel.

<highlight>Show the list of characters on the broadcast list<end>
<tab><symbol>broadcast

<highlight>Add a character to the broadcast list<end>
<tab><symbol>broadcast add 'character'

<highlight>Remove a character from the broadcast list<end>
<tab><symbol>broadcast rem 'character'
