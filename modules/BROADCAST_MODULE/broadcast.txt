To show the list of players on the broadcast list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>broadcast<end>

To add a player to the broadcast list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>broadcast add 'player'<end>

To remove a player from the broadcast list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>broadcast rem 'player'<end>

Note: the BROADCAST_MODULE allows you to receive tells from other bots and then relay those into your guild channel.
