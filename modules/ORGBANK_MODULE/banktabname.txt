<highlight> ::::: Org Bank: Bank Tab Help :::::: <end>

The 5 tabs of a Tabbed Bank can be renamed to almost anything you like:

<highlight>/tell <myname> !banktabname < 1-5 > <10 Characters><end>

Example: <highlight>!banktabname 1 Armour<end>

Example: <highlight>!banktabname 4 Weapons<end>

Example: <highlight>!banktabname 5 Spirits<end>

The Budabot shortcut for this is: <highlight>!btn < 1-5 > <10 Characters><end>
