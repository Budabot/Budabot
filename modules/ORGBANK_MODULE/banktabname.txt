<green><left> The 5 tabs of a Tabbed Bank can be renamed to almost anything you like:

<yellow><left>/tell <myname> !banktabname < 1-5 > <10 Characters><end>

Example:<yellow> !banktabname 1 Armour<end>

Example:<yellow> !banktabname 4 Weapons<end>

Example:<yellow> !banktabname 5 Spirits<end>

<green><left>The Budabot shortcut for this is: <yellow>!btn < 1-5 > <10 Characters><end>
