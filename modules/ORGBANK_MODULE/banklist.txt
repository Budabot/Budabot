<yellow><center>::::: Org Bank: Banklist Help :::::</center><end>

<green>Gives a complete list of all players who have Org Bank(s)<end> 

<green>Example: <yellow>/tell <myname> banklist <end> 

<green>When you click on the Search Results link, a list of players is displayed. 

You can then click on a players name to visit their Bank<end>

<green>The Budabot alias for this command is: <yellow>!bl<end>
