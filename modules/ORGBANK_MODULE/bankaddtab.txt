<header> :::::: Org Bank: Tabbed Bank Add Item Help :::::: <end>

<green>If you are using a <highlight>Tabbed<end> bank with a lot of items 
in it, this lets you put items into the other tabbed pages.<end>
 
<highlight>/tell <myname> bankadd tab<1 2 3 4 5> <drag item here> <optional: comment><end>

<green>Example:<end>

<highlight>/tell <myname> bankadd tab1 <drag item > LOAN ONLY<end>

<highlight>/tell <myname> bankadd tab3  <drag item > PACK 55<end>

<green><left>Once an item is in your Bank, you can add or delete it using the <grey>[-1|DELETE|+1] <green>buttons next to it.<end>

<green>The alias for this command is: <highlight>!bat <1-5> <drag item> <optional: comment><end><end>
