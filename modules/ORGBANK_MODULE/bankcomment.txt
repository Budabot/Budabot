<green>If you want to leave a comment or instructions for people to read when
they visit your Bank, this is where to look...

Example:<yellow> !bankcomment All items are for LOAN Only. <end>

Example:<yellow> !bankcomment I only play between 6pm and 12 pm GMT. <end>

Example:<yellow> !bankcomment Send tell to Elimeta to borrow these items. <end>

<green>To clear your bankcomment:<end> 

<yellow>/tell <myname> bankcomment none<end>

<green>The Budabot alias for this is: !bc < the text you want to say ><end>
