<yellow><center>::::: Org Bank: Basic Bank Add Item ::::::</center><end>

<green><left>If you are using a <yellow>Basic<green> bank to just dump a list of items you are prepared to lend, the command would be:
 
<yellow>/tell <myname> bankadd <drag item> <optional: comment><end>

<green>Examples:<end>

<yellow>/tell <myname> bankadd <drag item from inventory> LOAN ONLY<end>

<yellow>/tell <myname> bankadd <drag item from inventory> PACK 55<end>

<green><left>Once an item is in your Bank, you can add or delete it using the <grey>[-1|DELETE|+1) <green>buttons next to it.<end>

<green><left>The Budabot alias for this command is: <yellow>!ba <drag item> <optional: comment><end>
