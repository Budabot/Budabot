<header> :::::: Org Bank: Bankitem Help :::::: <end>

Searching for items in other members banks is easy. 

<highlight>/tell <myname> !bankitem < first few letters of the item ><end> 

Example: <highlight>/tell <myname> !bankitem cushion<end>

When you click on the Search Results link, a list of matched items is displayed. You can then click on a members name to visit their bank.

The Budabot alias for this is: <highlight>!bi < first few letters of item ><end>
