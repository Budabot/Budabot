<green>Searching for items in other members banks is easy.<end> 

<yellow>/tell <myname> !bankitem < first few letters of the item > <end> 

<green>Example:  <yellow>/tell <myname> !bankitem cushion<end>

<green><left>When you click on the Search Results link, a list of matched items is displayed. You can then click on a members name to visit their bank.<end>

<green>The Budabot alias for this is: <yellow>!bi < first few letters of item ><end>
