<header>::::: Help on Flip and Roll Commands. :::::<end>

<highlight>Flip Command<end>

To Flip a coin do:
<tab><symbol>flip

<highlight>Roll Command<end>

To roll a number between 1 and a endnumber:
<tab><symbol>roll 'Insert number'

To roll a number between a startvalue and a endvalue:
<tab><symbol>roll 'Insert startvalue' 'Insert endvalue'

<highlight>Verify Command<end>

To verify a roll
<tab><symbol>verify 'Insert Verifynumber'
