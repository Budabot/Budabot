To show a list of playfields, including their id, short name, and long name:
<highlight><tab><symbol>playfields<end>

To search for a specific playfield, append its short or long name:
<highlight><tab><symbol>playfields 'name'<end>

To create a waypoint link in chat:
<highlight><tab><symbol>waypoint 'x' 'y' 'playfield'<end>

Note: 'playfield' can be the playfield id, or any of the short or long names defined in <symbol>playfields
