<highlight>To show a list of playfields, including their id, short name, and long name:<end>
<tab><symbol>playfields

<highlight>To search for a specific playfield, append its short or long name:<end>
<tab><symbol>playfields 'name'

<highlight>To create a waypoint link in chat:<end>
<tab><symbol>waypoint 'x' 'y' 'playfield'

Note: 'playfield' can be the playfield id, or any of the short or long names defined in <symbol>playfields
