<highlight>To check if your Weapon/Armor/Bot is going over-equiped use<end>
<tab><symbol>oe 'insert needed skill here'
or
<tab><symbol>oe 'insert your skill here'

For example you have 112Pistol and wanna check what the max Pistol requirement is you can wear without going oe
<tab><symbol>oe 112

Another Example. If your favorite Weapon needs 1200Pistol and you wanna know how much pistol you need to not OE do:
<tab><symbol>oe 1200
