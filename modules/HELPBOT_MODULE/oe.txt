To check if your Weapon/Armor/Bot is over-equiped:
<highlight><tab><symbol>oe 'insert needed skill here'<end>
or
<highlight><tab><symbol>oe 'insert your skill here'<end>

For example you have 112 Pistol and wanna check what the max Pistol requirement is you can wear without going oe:
<highlight><tab><symbol>oe 112<end>

Another Example. If your favorite Weapon needs 1200 Pistol and you wanna know how much pistol you need to not OE do:
<highlight><tab><symbol>oe 1200<end>
