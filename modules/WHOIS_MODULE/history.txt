To show the history of a player:
<highlight><tab><symbol>history 'player'<end>

To show the history of a player on specific dimension:
<highlight><tab><symbol>history 'playername' 'dimension'<end>

Valid values for dimension are 1, 2, and 5:
1 - Atlantean
2 - Rimor
5 - New Server

(this will not work for characters on Die Neue Welt)
