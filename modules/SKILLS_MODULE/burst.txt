<symbol>burst [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>R<end>] [<orange>BD<end>] [<orange>BS<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Weapon Attack Time
[<orange>R<end>] = Weapon Recharge Time
[<orange>BD<end>] = Your Burst Delay*
[<orange>BS<end>] = Your Burst Skill

Example:
Your weapon has an attack time of <orange>1.2<end> seconds and a recharge time of <orange>1.5<end> seconds.  Your weapon has a Burst Delay* of <orange>1600<end>.  You have <orange>900<end> Burst skill.
<a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> burst 1.2 1.5 1600 900'><symbol>burst 1.2 1.5 1600 900</a>

* Your Burst Delay value (1600) can be found by using the <a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> help specials'>specials</a> command or on <a href='chatcmd:///start http://www.auno.org'>auno.org</a> as Burst Cycle.
