<header> :::::: Nanoinit Command :::::: <end>

/tell <myname> <symbol>nanoinit [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>I<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Nano Attack Time
[<orange>I<end>] = Your nano init skill

Example:
Your nano has an attack time (cast) of <orange>1.2<end> and your Nano Init skill is <orange>1200<end>.
<a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>nanoinit 1.2 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>nanoinit 1.2 1200</a>
