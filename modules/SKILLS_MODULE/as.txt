/tell <myname> <symbol>as [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>R<end>] [<orange>AS<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Weapon Attack Time
[<orange>R<end>] = Weapon Recharge Time
[<orange>AS<end>] = Your Aimed Shot skill

Example:
Your weapon has an attack time of <orange>1.2<end> seconds and a recharge time of <orange>1.5<end> seconds.  You have <orange>1200<end> Aimed Shot.
<a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>as 1.2 1.5 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>as 1.2 1.5 1200</a>
