<symbol>fullauto [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>R<end>] [<orange>FA Rech<end>] [<orange>FA Skill<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Weapon Attack Time
[<orange>R<end>] = Weapon Recharge Time
[<orange>FA Rech<end>] = Weapon Full Auto recharge value*
[<orange>FA Skill<end>] = Your Full Auto skill

Example:
Your weapon has an attack and recharge time of <orange>1<end> second and a Full Auto recharge value* of <orange>5000<end>.  You have <orange>1200<end> Full Auto skill.
<a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> fullauto 1 1 5000 1200'><symbol>fullauto 1 1 5000 1200</a>

* Your Full Auto recharge value (5000) can be found by using the <a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> help specials'>specials</a> command or on <a href='chatcmd:///start http://www.auno.org'>auno.org</a> as FullAuto Cycle.
