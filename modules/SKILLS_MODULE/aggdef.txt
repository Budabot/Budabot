<header>Agg/Def Weapon Init Usage:<end>
/tell <myname> <symbol>aggdef [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>R<end>] [<orange>I<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Weapon Attack Time
[<orange>R<end>] = Weapon Recharge Time
[<orange>I<end>] = Your weapon init skill
Example:
Your weapon has an attack time of <orange>1.2<end> seconds and a recharge time
of <orange>1.5<end> seconds.  Your melee/ranged init is <orange>1200<end>.
<a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>aggdef 1.2 1.5 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>aggdef 1.2 1.5 1200</a>
