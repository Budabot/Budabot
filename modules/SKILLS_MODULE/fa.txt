<header>Full Auto Recharge Usage:<end>
/tell <myname> <symbol>fa [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>R<end>] [<orange>FA Rech<end>] [<orange>FA Skill<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Weapon Attack Time
[<orange>R<end>] = Weapon Recharge Time
[<orange>FA Rech<end>] = Weapon Full Auto recharge value*
[<orange>FA Skill<end>] = Your Full Auto skill
Example:
Your weapon has an attack and recharge time of <orange>1<end> second and a 
Full Auto recharge value* of <orange>5000<end>.  You have <orange>1200<end> Full Auto skill.
<a href='chatcmd:///tell <myname> <symbol>fa 1 1 5000 1200'>/tell <myname> <symbol>fa 1 1 5000 1200</a>
* Your Full Auto recharge value (5000) can be found on <a href='chatcmd:///start http://www.auno.org'>auno.org</a> as FullAuto Cycle and <a href='chatcmd:///start http://aomainframe.net'>aomainframe.net</a> as FullAutoRecharge.
