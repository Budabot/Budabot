<header>fast Usage:<end>
/tell <myname> <symbol>fast [<orange>A<end>] [<orange>F<end>]
[<orange>A<end>] = Weapon Attack Time
[<orange>F<end>] = Your Fast Attack Skill
Example:
Your weapon has an attack time of <orange>1.2<end> seconds.
You have <orange>900<end> fast attack Skill.
