<header> :::::: Waitlist Functions :::::: <end>

<highlight>Show your waitlist<end>
<tab><symbol>waitlist

<highlight>Add a new player to your list<end>
<tab><symbol>waitlist add 'player name'

<highlight>Remove a player from your list<end>
<tab><symbol>waitlist rem 'player name'

<highlight>Remove all players from your list<end>
<tab><symbol>waitlist rem all

<highlight>Calling the next one from your list<end>
<tab><symbol>waitlist next
