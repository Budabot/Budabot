<header>::::: Raidlist :::::<end>

<red>Note<end>: The raidlist is created automatically after you do <symbol>raidstart. Everyone that is in the raidchannel is added.

Adding someone to the raidlist
<highlight><tab><symbol>raidadd 'name'<end>

Removing someone from the raidlist
<highlight><tab><symbol>raidkick 'name'<end>

Show the current raidlist
<highlight><tab><symbol>raidlist<end>

Check if everyone from the list is in the area
<highlight><tab><symbol>raidcheck<end>
