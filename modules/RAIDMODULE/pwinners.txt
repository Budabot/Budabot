<header>::::: Preserve Winners :::::<end>

Preserve Winners mode means that a person can only win one item per raid.

<highlight>To see the current status<end>
<tab><symbol>pwinners

<highlight>Enabling the mode<end>
<tab><symbol>pwinners on

<highlight>Disabling the mode<end>
<tab><symbol>pwinners off

<highlight>Reseting the list<end>
<tab><symbol>pwinners clear
