Hold Hell at Bay                      

kill 2 dragons:    
- Xark the Battletoad (in the volcano NNE of Yutto Marshes - Just follow the Road)    

- Razor the Battletoad (in the volcano NW of Yutoo Marshes - Easiest way is to just swim across the lava to the west side of the ramp)    

Xark drops 3x Xark's Whisker each kill and Razor drops 3x Razor's Fang2. kill Tchu and give the Whisker and the Fang to Nhohar to get your Hold Hell at Bay. (see !info Tchu for more information on this step)
