<header> :::::: Private and Admin News :::::: <end>

This is intended for Raidbots where the admin wants to set News like Botupdates etc.

Privnews will be shown to all non-admin characters when they join the bot.
Adminnews will be shown to only admins when they join the bot.

<highlight>Set news for normal players
<tab><symbol>privnews 'text'

<highlight>Clear news for normal players
<tab><symbol>privnews clear

<highlight>Set news for admins
<tab><symbol>adminnews 'text'

<highlight>Clear news for admins
<tab><symbol>adminnews clear
