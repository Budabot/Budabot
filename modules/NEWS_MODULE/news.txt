<highlight>To show the latest news entries:<end>
<tab><symbol>news

<highlight>To add a news entry:<end>
<tab><symbol>news add 'text'

<highlight>To sticky or unsticky a news post:<end>
<tab><symbol>news set sticky 'news_id_#'
<tab><symbol>news set unsticky 'news_id_#'

<highlight>To delete a news entry click on the 'Remove' link next to the news entry you want to remove<end>
