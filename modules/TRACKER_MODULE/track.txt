<header> ::::: Track Commands ::::: <end>

<highlight>To see the list of users on the track list:<end>
<tab><symbol>track

<highlight>To see the track history for a specific player:<end>
<tab><symbol>track player

<highlight>To add a player to the track history:<end>
<tab><symbol>track add player

<highlight>To remove a player from the track history:<end>
<tab><symbol>track rem player
