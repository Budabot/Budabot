<header> :::::: Bank Command :::::: <end>

<highlight>To list the bank characters in the database:<end>
<tab><symbol>bank browse

<highlight>to list the backpacks of a particular bank character:<end>
<tab><symbol>bank browse 'character'

<highlight>To see the contents of a backpack on a bank character<end>
<tab><symbol>bank browse 'character' 'backpackname'

<highlight>To search for an item on a bank character<end>
<tab><symbol>bank search 'items to search for'
