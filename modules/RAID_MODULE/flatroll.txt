To add an item to the roll list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot 'item name'<end>
or
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot 'item reference'<end>

To add an item multiple times to the roll list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>multiloot #x 'item reference'<end>
(e.g.: <symbol>multiloot 3x Lockpick)

To remove an item from the roll list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>remloot 'item number'<end>

To clear the roll list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot clear<end>

To show the roll list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>loot<end>

To roll the roll list:
<highlight><tab><symbol>flatroll<end>

To re-add remaining items after a roll:
<highlight><tab><symbol>reroll<end>
