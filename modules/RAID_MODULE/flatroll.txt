<header> :::::: Flatrolling Items :::::: <end>

<highlight>Adding an item to the list<end>
<tab><symbol>loot 'item name'
or
<tab><symbol>loot 'item reference'

<tab><symbol>multiloot #x 'item reference' (e.g.: <symbol>multiloot 3x Lockpick)

<highlight>Removing an item from the list<end>
<tab><symbol>remloot 'item number'

<highlight>Clearing the list<end>
<tab><symbol>loot clear

<highlight>Show the loot list<end>
<tab><symbol>list

<highlight>Rolling the items<end>
<tab><symbol>flatroll

<highlight>Re-adding remaining items after a roll<end>
<tab><symbol>reroll
