<header>::::: Alien Playfield Loottable :::::<end>

<highlight>To see the loot of a specific sector do<end>
<tab><symbol>loottable 'Sector number'
<tab>(Allowed Sector numbers are 13, 28 and 35)

For example
<tab><symbol>loottable 13
shows what drops from the Generals in Sector 13 and which use they have.
