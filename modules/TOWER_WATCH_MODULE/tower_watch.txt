<header>::::: Tower Watch Commands :::::<end>

<highlight>Shows info on all the sites
<tab><symbol>opentimes


<highlight>Adds a site to the watch list
<tab><symbol>scout &lt;zone&gt; &lt;base number&gt; &lt;closing time&gt; &lt;CT QL&gt; &lt;Omni|Clan|Neutral&gt; &lt;org name&gt;

<tab>eg: <symbol>scout PW 2 16:55:28 57 Omni Insanity-inc


<highlight>Removes a site from the watch list
<tab><symbol>remscout &lt;zone&gt; &lt;base number&gt;

<tab>eg: <symbol>remscout PW 2
