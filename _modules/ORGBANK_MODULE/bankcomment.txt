<header> :::::: Org Bank: Bank Comment Help :::::: <end>

If you want to leave a comment or instructions for people to read when
they visit your Bank, this is where to look...

Example: <highlight>!bankcomment All items are for LOAN Only.<end>

Example: <highlight>!bankcomment I only play between 6pm and 12 pm GMT.<end>

Example: <highlight>!bankcomment Send tell to Elimeta to borrow these items.<end>

To clear your bankcomment: 

<highlight>/tell <myname> bankcomment none<end>

The Budabot alias for this is: <highlight>!bc < the text you want to say ><end>
