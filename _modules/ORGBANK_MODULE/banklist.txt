<header> :::::: Org Bank: Banklist Help :::::: <end>

Gives a complete list of all players who have Org Bank(s)

Example: <highlight>/tell <myname> banklist<end> 

When you click on the Search Results link, a list of players is displayed. 

You can then click on a players name to visit their Bank

The Budabot alias for this command is: <highlight>!bl<end>
